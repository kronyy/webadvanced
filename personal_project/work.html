Loris Azzaro Founder of Azzaro

Loris Azzaro was an Italian-French fashion designer and the founder of the Azzaro brand. Born in 1933 in Tunis, Tunisia, he later moved to Paris where he built his fashion empire. He became known for his glamorous, elegant, and bold designs that captured the spirit of luxury and confidence.

In 1967, Azzaro launched his fashion house, quickly gaining popularity among celebrities and high-profile clients. His designs were often worn by stars, helping him establish a reputation for sophistication and sensuality.

Beyond fashion, Loris Azzaro expanded into fragrances, creating iconic scents that reflect his signature style—seductive, modern, and timeless. The Azzaro fragrance line continues to be popular worldwide, carrying on his legacy of elegance and charisma.

Loris Azzaro passed away in 2003, but his influence remains strong in the fashion and fragrance industries. His brand continues to represent confidence, boldness, and refined luxury.
